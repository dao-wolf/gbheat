With many years of experience in the gas engineering industry, at GBHeat we offer competitive prices for gas installation and maintenance to customers throughout local London areas.

We provides individual households with a professional gas installation and maintenance service of both gas central heating boiler systems and gas appliance installations.

The service is designed to meet Landlord letting property regulations as well as for all domestic properties, used on an annual basis at least once, to ensure the longevity and validity of your boiler installations.

It is recommended that gas boilers get serviced once a year in order to prevent carbon monoxide poisoning and to keep your hot water and central heating working efficiently.

We can issue gas safety reports and service your boiler.
